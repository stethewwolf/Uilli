formato:

x1/y1/x2/y2/du/dd/dl/dr/back/guide/start/TL/TR/A/B/X/Y/LB/RB/LT/RT

significati:
 - x1, y1 = joystick sx
 - x2, y2 = joystick dx
 - du, dd, dl, dr = pad up/down/left/right
 - back, guide, start = other
 - TL, TR = Trigger joystick sx, dx
 - A, B, X, Y = pulsanti
 - LB, RB = pulsanti dietro sx, dx
 - LT, RT = pulsanti dietro analogici sx, dx

value:
 - x1, x2, y1, y2 =
 - du, dd, dl, dr, back, guide, start, TL, TR, A, B, X, Y, LB, RB = 0-1
 - LT, RT = 0 -> 255
